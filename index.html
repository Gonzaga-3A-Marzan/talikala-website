All About Talikala

Talikala's Mission

Talikala strives tdo aid women to break free from the chains of oppression. It aids in changing society’s views on prostitution, a view that has festered from male supremacy and misogyny. It aims to improve the lives of women who are survivors of sex trafficking and prostitution and empower them as well.

Beneficiaries/Benefactors

Beneficiaries - The people who are being admitted in the institution are women who are enslaved by the oppressive chains of society via living lives of prostitution. Women who are having a hard time to be accepted by the society due to their traumatic past, yet striving to for a better future for theirselves. Benefactors - People and organizations who agree to have a proper partnership with talikala for promotion and help.

Brief Summary

Talikala is a non-profitable organization that supports and help prostituted women in Davao City. They want to empower as many individuals who have suffered the same. Although, due to financial circumstances, they have limited access in doing things to serve the community.
