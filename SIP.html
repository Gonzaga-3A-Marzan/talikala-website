Title:

The Effect of Coffee Grounds on the Height, Number of Leaves, and Pest Infestations of Allium fistulosu(Green onion)

Problem Statement:

What is the effect of adding coffee grounds as fertilizer on the height, number of leaves, and pest infestations of Allium fistulosu (green onions)?

Hypothesis

If we add coffee grounds in the growth of our Allium Fistulosu(green onions), then the green onions will grow taller and will have more leaves, and will have no bug infestations.

Controlled Variables:

Size of the pot, amount of water, type of water, amount of sunlight, type of pot, amount of soil, type of soil, and number of spring onions per pot.

Independent and Dependent Variables:

Independent:
Amount of the compost fertilizer (coffee grounds)
Dependent:
The height of the plant, the number of leaves produced, and the number of bug infestations observed.
